Our Programs

To achieve our goals, we have the following programs

Guided by SDG 5 (Gender Equality), SDG 12 (Responsible Production and Consumption), and SDG 13 (Climate Action), Mviringo Africa is dedicated to transforming communities through sustainable solutions. We envision a world where communities thrive in harmony with nature, empowered by sustainable practices.

Climate Conversations

Monthly discussions and forums to engage the community in meaningful conversations about climate change and sustainability. This program fosters open, judgment-free spaces for learning about climate change and taking action. Help us design the toolkit by filling out our Google form.

Recycling Drives

We are planning our first Recycling Drive to collect recyclable materials, reduce waste, and promote the importance of recycling. These drives will provide drop-off points for items like paper, plastic, glass, and electronics. Volunteer at our first Recycling Drive here
