About Us

Empowering Change, Greening Africa's Future.

Mviringo Africa is on a mission to foster a cleaner, greener, and more sustainable future for Africa by engaging schools, communities, businesses, and individuals in initiatives that promote environmental stewardship, education, and innovation.

  • Advocating for and implementing circular economy solutions to rethink consumption habits..
  • Uniting communities and businesses to combat plastic pollution and its environmental consequences.
  • Empowering individuals through education and innovation to become environmental change-makers.
Our Strategies

To fulfill our goal, we employ these approaches

Mviringo Africa is focused on implementing the following four strategies to combat the issue of plastic pollution within the Catholic University of East Africa community and its surrounding area

-

Monthly cleanups

Monthly cleanups around the school and its sorroundings.

Strategic bin placement

Strategic bin placement within the university for plastics.

Educational workshops

Educational workshops on the concept of a circular economy.

Promoting Sustainable Solutions

Assisting small businesses with transitioning from plastic packaging to sustainable and biodegradable alternatives.
